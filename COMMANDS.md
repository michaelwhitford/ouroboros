# Commands

Commands emerge from the system. Find what is already there. Encoding commands hurts more than it helps. Try multiple ways to say the same thing to find useful words and combos.

## General

`game mode` or `engage` or `collapse`

`explain the game to me`

`show me your goal ladder brief`

`show me your decision tree brief`

`engage and self-evaluate`

`engage and recover from the last session`

## Modifiers

Words you can append to affect output.

`brief` `discuss` `debug` `explain` `compact`

Words that affect working modes.

`analyze` `debug` `troubleshoot` `explain` `discuss` `atomic` `only use line edits`

## Explore these words.

`patterns` `workflows` `learning` `evolve` `graph` `tree` `query` `connections`

## Meta

`how can we encode new workflows and patterns?  explain and discuss`
